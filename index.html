Navigate the latent space of your trained models

A local toolkit for exploring StyleGAN2 models trained on your own images. Walk through the latent space frame by frame, browse a 2D PCA projection of your model, or pan infinitely through a continuous latent map — all running on your own GPU, no cloud required.
